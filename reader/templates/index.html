How sutta IDs work

Most IDs are a nikāya prefix + number:

dn22Dīgha Nikāya · sutta 22
mn128Majjhima Nikāya · sutta 128
sn56.11Saṁyutta Nikāya · saṁyutta 56, sutta 11
an3.65Aṅguttara Nikāya · nipāta 3, sutta 65

Khuddaka Nikāya books — usually verse-numbered:

dhp1-20Dhammapada · verses 1–20 (range)
snp1.8Sutta Nipāta · vagga 1, sutta 8
iti85Itivuttaka · verse 85
mil3.1.1Milindapañha · 3.1.1

Vinaya — descriptive prefixes, dash-separated:

pli-tv-bu-pmPāli · Theravāda · Bhikkhu · Pātimokkha
pli-tv-bu-vb-pj1… Bhikkhu · Vibhaṅga · Pārājika 1
pli-tv-kd1… Khandhaka 1 (Mahāvagga)
pli-tv-pvr10… Parivāra 10

Abhidhamma — chapter-based:

vb1Vibhaṅga · ch. 1
kv10.1Kathāvatthu · kathā 10, subsection 1
patthana1.1Paṭṭhāna · section 1, subsection 1

A :n.m suffix on any ID points to a specific segment — e.g. mn128:1.1 = segment 1.1 of MN 128. Cross-reference URLs from MCP tools use this form to deep-link a quoted line.
